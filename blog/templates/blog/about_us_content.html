Our Mission

We are not alone

Harassment, discrimination, politics, and other conflicts in the workplace inspired us to realize that many other women could be facing similar situations in their workplaces.

We want to listen

Interviewing and collecting cases from women who have undergone unfavorable incidents in academia or industry is our mission. We want to highlight the real, the here, and the now in the hopes of illuminating truth in the darkest places.

We want to work with you

By bringing awareness to universities, corporations, and other organizational entities, our “Empower Now” program intends to prevent inappropriate incidents from occuring in the workplace. Inspiring empathy in every user is key to preparing them to respond aptly to on-the-field situations.

We want to speak up

Our published cases aim to empower women, particularly those in STEM fields, to speak up--whether it be to their perpetrator, manager, organizational authority, or themselves. We want to help women embrace their emotions, discern the best path forward, and, ultimately, heal from their incidents.

We want you to stick with it

More than 200 women in top, tech firm positions reported sexist work interactions, with one in three women having felt afraid for their safety [1]. Additionally,, nearly 40 percent who earn engineering degrees either quit or never enter the profession [2]. Thus, our mission is simple: to help women joyfully pursue their STEM dreams without feeling uncomfortable, inhibited, or dissuaded in any way because of uncalled for people-play.
